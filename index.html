Founder Story

The visionaries behind Education Trust launched this initiative with a simple belief: every child deserves an inspiring and supportive education. With decades of experience in teaching and school leadership, they brought together a team committed to excellence, compassion, and community engagement. Under their guidance, our collective of schools has grown into a nurturing environment that balances academic rigor with creativity and character development. Their hands-on approach and emphasis on values remain central to our mission across all locations.
